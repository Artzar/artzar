HANNA SCOTT is a graduate of Concordia University's Communication Studies, Montreal. Her work has been published in Europe, Canada and the U.S. She has exhibited in a group show at the Blenheim Gallery, London. Scott currently resides in Toronto and is affiliated with the Printziples Fine Art Gallery.
