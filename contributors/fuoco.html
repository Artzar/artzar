BONNIE FUOCO studied painting at Parsons School of Design in New York. She has exhibited in numerous solo and group shows including the Provincetown Art Association, the West Virginia Governor's Mansion, and the Univeristy of Charleston, West Virginia. In 1996 she received a Terra Foundation Fellowship and painted and exhibited in Giverny, France. She lives in Lewisburg, West Virginia.
