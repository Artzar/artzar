MIKE MCCAFFREY is the left brain behind Artzar, and is responsible for the most of the design and coding on this site. He is using this opportunity to develop an appreciation of art that he somehow never absorbed while pursuing a degree in computer science and engineering. He spends his days trying alleviate the problems of the world using his technical skills and extreme rationality, but he hasn't quite figured out exactly how to go about doing so yet. If he does, you will be the first to know. Some people claim his photographs are quite nice, but he refuses to believe them.

Mike would like to thank everyone else who has made this site what it is - namely his co-conspirator Mark who started this project and brings in all the content, and all of the contributors, without whose great art none of this would be possible.
