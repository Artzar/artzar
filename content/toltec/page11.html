Beginning the Climb, Pyramid of the Sun
"The last chakra, The Pyramid of the Sun, is actually a chakra above your head, where you've moved out of the earthly sphere. This is where you make an act of gratitude towards the Universe. You acknowledge how grateful you are to be alive. And as a human being, we're unique. We stand between the earth and the sun, as a life form. And we are only one of the life forms that has the ability to acknowledge that it's standing in that place. That we're not just of the earth and we're not just of spirit. We have this unique ability to acknowledge that. And on this last day of the journey, you send your goodwill to the Universe, acknowledging all of the gifts that you've received. It's kind of a thanksgiving, a summarization of whatever insight or knowledge you've received during the five days in a posture of gratitude."
