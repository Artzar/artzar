1st Chola
"There was a man in the group who got horribly sunburned and one of the teachers said, I'm going to pick out a hat for you. And he made him wear this ridiculously-looking sombrero. He was heckled by the locals but he said it actually helped him deal with his 'self-importance' issues. He said that it really changed his consciousness. Having to wear something that made him look like a clown made him look at how he wanted to be perceived."
